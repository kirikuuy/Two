ABOUT US


Our products are the result of the hard work and dedication of our team of experts, specialized in different fields, who constantly follow the newest trends in order to develop

products able to meet customer’s needs. To make sure we keep our quality at the highest level, we have innovated laboratory, responsible for monitoring each phase of the production process.

SIL Chemicals Ltd was early incorporated in 2006 formerly known as Showelux Industries ltd since 1996. SIL is a private company limited by shares incorporated under the

companies Act of 2002.

Since its establishment the company has engaged its activities as Chemicals manufacturer, importer and distributor working together in partnership with different

international companies serving all Tanzania Markets.

For the past 12 years, SIL Chemicals has provided an extensive range of innovative chemical products and services catering for the Professional and Industrial usage.

Today, SIL Chemicals is one of the nation’s largest distributors, importer and manufacturer of all range of industrial and chemical detergents.

With over 25 staff, SIL Chemicals has successfully established wholesale, retailers and industrial outlets nationwide to accommodate the needs of its customer

Our success is primarily based on customizing client requirements to create true commercial value for the client. This fundamental principal drives high client loyalty and in

turn focuses our joint attention towards:

  • Reducing Client Procurement Costs.

  • Reducing Client Transaction Costs

  • Share client field innovations

  • Ensure quality control is Maintained

  • Improving Client/End User Relations

  • Environmental sustainability and safety is our key factor
